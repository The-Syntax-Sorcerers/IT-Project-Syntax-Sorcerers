Course Description:

In recent years, quantum computing has emerged as a game-changing technology with the potential to solve complex problems that are practically infeasible for classical computers. COMP123456 offers students an in-depth understanding of quantum computing concepts, algorithms, and their real-world applications. Through a combination of lectures, hands-on labs, and interactive discussions, students will journey from the foundational principles of quantum mechanics to advanced quantum algorithms and programming techniques.
